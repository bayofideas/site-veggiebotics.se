Our Philosophy

Built, not bought

Pre-built appliances are designed for the lowest common denominator. They hide complexity behind marketing promises and warranty limitations. We believe in transparency. When you build something yourself, you own it completely.

Precision over convenience

Consumer products optimize for convenience. We optimize for control. Every parameter adjustable. Every sensor accessible. Every decision yours to make.

Software-defined growing

Hardware is the foundation. Software is where intelligence lives. Our platform evolves continuously, adding capabilities without requiring you to buy new equipment.

Open by default

APIs, not black boxes. Documentation, not secrets. We build for people who want to understand, modify, and extend. Your data. Your system. Your way.
