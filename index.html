What is Veggiebotics?

Not an appliance. A system. Veggiebotics delivers precision-engineered DIY kits that let you build, customize, and control your own automated growing environment.

Precision Hardware

Lab-grade sensors, industrial pumps, and custom PCBs. Every component selected for reliability and precision.

Software Control

Veggiebotics OS gives you full control. Monitor, automate, and optimize through our dashboard or API.

AI Intelligence

Machine learning models predict growth, optimize nutrients, and detect issues before they become problems.
